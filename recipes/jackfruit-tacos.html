Jackfruit Tacos

Description

Enjoy a fun night with your peers with easy to make snack.

This vegan friendly twist is sure to to win over your friends and can be made in approx 10 minutes for last minute get togethers. Starting with the stove the ingredients get fried together until placed upon a shell. Topped with jalapeno, salsa and cheese your mouth will have a variety of flavours to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 4 taco shells
  • 2 cans of jackfruit
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable Oil
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1 sliced jalapeno
  • 1/2 cup of salsa
  • 1/2 cup of vegan cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a sauce pan over medium heat.
  2. Add jackfruit while stiring for 2 minutes.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of oil and taco seasoning sting for 1 minute.
  4. Serve with jalapeno, salsa and vegan cheese.
